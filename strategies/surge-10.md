# Strategy: Surge-10

## Intuition
Between 09:00 and 10:00, a subset of stocks exhibits repeated intraday surges from +10% to +15%. I define a monitoring universe of stocks that are up at least 5%. An entry signal is triggered when the price reaches +10%, and the position is exited at +15%.

## Hypothesis
People expect some stocks to surge and hit limit up Market activity and volatility peak between 9:00 and 10:00
